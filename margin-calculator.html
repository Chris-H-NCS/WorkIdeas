Notes

Margin % = ((Sell Price - Cost Price) / Sell Price) * 100
Cost Price = Sell Price * (1 - (Margin % / 100))
Sell Price = Cost Price / (1 - (Margin % / 100))
Margin % must be less than 100 when calculating Sell Price or Cost Price.
